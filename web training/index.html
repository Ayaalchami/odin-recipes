THE ODIN RECIPES

The baked rolls

ingredients:

  • rolls
  • chicken
  • oil

steps:

  1. put the rolls on a clean table
  2. put the oil on it then add the chicken
  3. turn the oven on and put the rolls for 30min
  4. take it out of the oven and enjoy!

THE ODIN RECIPES

The fried chicken

ingredients:

  • green onion
  • chicken
  • oil

steps:

  1. put the chicken in the tray
  2. put the oil on it then add the green onion
  3. turn the oven on and put the tray for 20min
  4. take it out of the oven and enjoy!
